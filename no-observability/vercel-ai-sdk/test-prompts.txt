Wonder Toys — Sample Test Prompts
===================================

SEARCH — FREE TEXT (searchProducts: query)
-------------------------------------------
1.  What toys do you have for babies?
2.  I'm looking for something educational for my 7-year-old
3.  Do you sell anything to do with science or experiments?
4.  Show me your best outdoor toys
5.  I want a toy that helps with creativity

SEARCH — BY CATEGORY (searchProducts: category)
-------------------------------------------------
6.  What building toys do you carry?
7.  Show me all your developmental toys
8.  What's in the adventure category?

SEARCH — BY AGE RANGE (searchProducts: minAge/maxAge)
------------------------------------------------------
9.  What toys are suitable for a 3-year-old?
10. What do you have for a newborn?
11. What do you have for kids aged 8 to 12?
12. Show me toys for teenagers
13. What's appropriate for a 5-year-old but not too babyish?

SEARCH — KEYWORDS
-----------------

19. I'm looking for something with STEM or coding
20. Do you carry anything related to motor skills?
